The Roam Way

From first enquiry to lasting friendship.

Every Roam journey begins long before departure and carries on long after you return home.

1
Explore

Browse journeys, read the itinerary, understand what's included. Take your time.

2
Consult

A short, personal call with us. We answer your questions and make sure it's the right fit.

3
Book

£500 deposit secures your place. Balance due 8 weeks before departure. No surprises.

4
Before We Roam

A pre-departure gathering in London. Meet the group, meet your host, and begin the journey before you leave.

5
After We Roam

Six weeks after you return, we gather again. Photo album, shared stories, and the next adventure on the horizon.
