The Journey

Roll through ancient cities, vineyards and unhurried afternoons.

Tuscany rewards the curious. This journey moves through Renaissance Florence, walled Siena, rolling vineyard estates and the quiet of hilltop villages — with a pace designed to let places sink in.

Your local host — a cultural guide with deep knowledge of the region — will take you beyond what guidebooks reach. Private visits, real conversations, long tables shared with people who care about the same things you do.

Roam Well handles every detail. You simply arrive, connect, and explore.

Common Questions

Answers before you ask.

We've answered the questions guests most often have. If yours isn't here, get in touch.

I'm travelling solo — will I feel out of place?
Many Roam Well guests travel solo. Our groups are deliberately small and the structured social moments — Before We Roam, welcome dinner, shared experiences — mean connections form naturally and quickly.
How active are the days?
Gentle to moderate. We walk, explore and move through the journey — but nothing strenuous. Afternoons are frequently free, and pace is never rushed. This journey is designed to be absorbed, not ticked.
Can I have my own room?
Yes — a private room is included in the standard price for all guests. There's no shared accommodation on any Roam Well journey.
What if I need support during the trip?
Your host is with the group throughout. Our team in London is also contactable at any point. We take duty of care seriously — it's part of how we're built.
I've never done a group trip before. Is this for me?
This is a question many of our guests ask. The short answer: Roam Well groups are nothing like packaged tours. Small, thoughtfully matched, with built-in connection from The Lounge onwards.
What's the typical age range?
Our travellers are broadly in the 50–70 range, though we don't restrict by age. What unites them is curiosity, a desire for depth, and the sense that the best chapters of travel are still ahead.
Are children welcome on Roam Well journeys?
Roam Well journeys are strictly adults only — all guests must be 18 or over. This isn't a footnote; it's central to what we do. Our journeys are designed around the idea that this time is entirely yours. To be spontaneous, to linger over dinner, to follow your curiosity without compromise. Every guest travels for themselves, and the group dynamic that makes Roam Well special depends on it.
