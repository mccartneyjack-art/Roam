Our Story

Travel should feel like
it was made for you.

Vagano began with a shared belief that travel should be richer, deeper, and more personal than what was on offer.

Where It Began

A gap worth
closing.

Antonio and Jack have spent years travelling — independently, curiously, and with a genuine hunger to understand the places they visited rather than simply pass through them. Between them they've navigated enough countries, enough cultures and enough travel operators to know exactly what's missing from most of the options on the market.

The best travel experiences they'd had shared something in common: a knowledgeable local who opened doors that guidebooks couldn't, a group small enough to feel like friends by the second day, and an itinerary with enough structure to feel confident but enough space to feel free.

That combination — reliably, accessibly, without compromise — didn't exist as a product. So they built it.

"The best journeys come from people who genuinely know and love where they're taking you."

Antonio & Jack — Co-Founders

The Founders

Two friends.
One shared conviction.

Antonio and Jack have spent years travelling and understand the ins and outs of what makes a journey genuinely memorable — and what lets one down. Roam is built on that first-hand knowledge, applied with the care and rigour that the experience deserves.

Antonio
Co-Founder

He founded Roam to make that quality of experience consistently available — not as a luxury exception, but as the standard. He oversees every itinerary and every host relationship personally.

Jack

What We Stand For

The principles behind
every journey.

Roam is built on a set of beliefs about what travel should feel like. They shape every decision — from how we choose destinations to how we select hosts to who we welcome into each group.

All Roam journeys are strictly adults only. This is intentional and central to the experience — a space designed entirely around the adults taking it, with no compromise on pace, depth or spontaneity.

01
Depth over breadth

We'd rather spend two nights in one place and truly understand it than tick off five in a week. Every itinerary is built around immersion, not coverage.

02
Small by conviction

Eight to ten guests. Never more. Small enough that the host knows everyone by name, and everyone knows each other by the second morning.

03
Local, not logistical

Our hosts aren't guides with itineraries. They're people who live where they take you — who know which trattorian the locals actually go to, and which to politely ignore.

04
Confidence, not compromise

Structure that gives freedom, not restrictions. Guests arrive knowing what to expect, and leave with the confidence to roam further on their own terms.

05
Your time, entirely yours

Adults only. No compromises, no counting heads, no adjusting the pace for anyone but the people in the room. This trip belongs to you.

I
We will always be honest about pricing

No hidden fees, no optional extras that make the headline price meaningless. What you see is what you pay. Single rooms are included as standard.

II
We will only run trips we would take ourselves

That's the test. Every host, every hotel, every experience is held to it. If we wouldn't personally recommend it to someone we cared about, it doesn't go in the itinerary.

III
We will keep groups small, always

Ten guests maximum. We will never grow a departure to fill a coach. The intimate scale of Roam is the product — we won't trade it for volume.
